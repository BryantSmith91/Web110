Webpage Review: Towards Data Science

  • What is the company/organization?

    The Parent Company is Medium

  • What is the URL

    https://towardsdatascience.com/

  • What is the purpose of the site?

    The purpose of this website is to share and keep readers updated on new developments in Data Science programming

  • Do you believe the site reaches its audience?

    By virtue of having a large backlog of articles on different strategies and tutorials for Data Science and the website’s Search Engine Optimization it does reach it’s audience well.

  • Was the site useful to you? Why or why not?

    In the past, the site has been very useful to me by providing tutorials, examples, and information on Python Extensions and Data Science methods.

  • List the colors and/or graphics that are used on the home page of this website, etc.

    Colors are Blue, White, Black and Gray. Images are Company logo (Medium), an image for each article on the homepage and user/writer icons.
